MARABU A CAMP

about us

Located in the Heart of the Rift Valley just North of Kajiado,

overlooking the Ol Donyo Orok Mountain and a view of Mount Kilimanjaro and Mount Meru on a clear day.

MARABU A HOTEL is an escape from the busy city life and an immersion into the heart of nature.

Sleep under canvas and wake up to the melody of the birds. A true digiral detox! Interact up close with the amazing wildlife Kenya has to offer like Grant Gazelles, Impalas, Kudus, Elands, Ostriches, Wildebeests and a variety of exotic birds .Our animal sanctuary is embedded within stunning landscapes which guests can view from the comfort of their cottage balcony. You can do a little yoga with the stunning view of Mt. Kilimanjaro and enjoy the view of the acacia treetops all the way up to our famous hill the Oldonyo Orok mountain while sitting by the pool and enjoying your favourite cocktail. A quiet environment for guests looking to unwind from daily hussle and stress of a busy city life. The camp accommodates only 20 guests at most on a 50 acre facility providing enough privacy for every guest. Our experienced chef boosts in sourcing the freshest ingredients from our organic garden and presenting them in the most mouthwatering of ways. It is the perfect hillside getaway for families and friends bringing you closer physically and emotionally. We are here to provide you with an authentic experience between travelers, nature and culture.

We believe that sustainability is the future,

so we have reduced our consumption of electricity, everything on-site is powered by solar energy and we try to limit both our guests and our unnecessary water consumption.

Include a night or two stop over on your travel to Amboseli National Park, Mt. Kilimanjaro or Ngorongoro crater.
